Poems by Monique Soria[see more of Monique's work here]

Monique Soria’s poems have been published in:
  • This Piece of Earth: Images and Words from Tumamoc Hill
  • Spiral Orb
    • Five: A Poetic Inventory of Saguaro National Park(Spring 2012)
    • Twelve(Fall 2016)
  • Journal of the Southwest Volume 57 Summer-Autumn 2015
She was one of 20 poets whose haikus were in Tucson’s Old Pueblo Poems public art installation, Haiku Hike, in 2020 (inaugural) and 2022. Monique was born in Cleveland, Ohio, where her father came from Bolivia to study and her mother’s family came from Belgium after World War II. Monique lives near Tucson, Arizona. She is Zoe Freilich's aunt.
